Terms & Conditions

This document was created by the organizing team of Darwin 2020 on account of the international conference being held from 2nd to 5th December, 2020.

All these rules and regulations assure authenticity and induce efficiency in the field of practical sciences in order to maintain proper order on all levels with minimal or no tolerance for misbehaviour. The rules and regulations mentioned below may be applied by the organizing team as and when required so and wherever needed so as to achieve the objective.

Respect: for individuals, for data and for the community.

It is important to understand that each attendee present needs to behave with a certain code of conduct that includes basic courtesy which involves respect for co-attendees, for speakers, for the organizing team and other members of the bio-community, for their data and for the objectives of the conference. Failure to do so will result in immediate disbarment of the member/ participant who violates the ethical code of conduct.

Privacy:

If any attendee is found recording any part of the event without prior consent/permission of the organizing team, there maybe a sequence of legal actions that may be taken against the attendee. Recording of any part of the event is strictly prohibited and failure to follow this rule will result in heavy consequences.
